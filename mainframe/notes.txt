Port 3270 is the classic IBM 3270 terminal protocol (usually exposed as TN3270 — “Telnet 3270”). It’s the mainframe display/terminal protocol (screen-oriented, not a plain-line shell like SSH). You’ll need a 3270-capable client (x3270 / s3270 / tn3270) to get a proper terminal session.
